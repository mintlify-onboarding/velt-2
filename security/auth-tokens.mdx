---
title: "Generating Auth Tokens"
---




<Steps>
<Step title="Go to the Velt Console">

Open the Velt Console at [console.velt.dev](https://console.velt.dev)

</Step>
<Step title="Click Generate Token button">

Under the Auth Token section, click on Generate Token button:

![](https://files.readme.io/78efa84-image.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Go to the Velt Console">

Copy the generated token.

![](https://files.readme.io/97c5e80-image.png)

</Step>
<Step title="Save the token">

Click on Save button to save the token.

</Step>


</Steps>
